How to Use TextTool

A simple guide to help you use our text tools efficiently

What is TextTool?

TextTool is an online utility that helps you edit, clean, format, and convert text quickly without installing any software.

Available Features

  • Text formatting & cleaning
  • Case conversion (Uppercase, Lowercase, Sentence case)
  • Word & character counter
  • Download text as PDF or TXT
  • Fast & secure processing

Step-by-Step Usage

Step 1

Paste or type your text into the input box.

Step 2

Select the operation you want to perform.

Step 3

Review the processed output instantly.

Step 4

Download or copy the final result.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use clean plain text for accurate results
  • Check formatting before downloading
  • Use dark mode for long editing sessions
